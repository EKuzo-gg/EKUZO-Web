Training Progression

1

Learn to Play (Mechanics)

Wave management, trading windows, jungle tracking, and optimizing your settings. The foundation of high-level play.

2

Find Your Role (Specialization)

Deep dives into champion pools, matchups, and your specific job within the team composition. Stop playing everything, start mastering one thing.

3

Team Play (Macro)

Drafting, objective control, cross-map plays, and playing weakside. Moving from solo queue chaos to coordinated strategy.

4

Real Life Skills (Mental)

Effective comms (no flaming), tilt management, leadership, and giving/receiving constructive feedback. Skills that translate outside the game.

5

Compete (Showcase)

Friday tournament day. Put the week's practice to the test against other camp squads in a casted, competitive environment.
