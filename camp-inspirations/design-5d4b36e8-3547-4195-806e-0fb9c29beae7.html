STAGE SELECT: CURRICULUM

A structured 5-day progression balancing mechanics with teamwork.

DAY 1
LEARN TO PLAY
Master the fundamentals. Map awareness, wave management, and economy basics. Establish the baseline for competitive play.
DAY 2
FIND YOUR ROLE
Champion specialization. 1-on-1 coaching to identify your strengths. Are you a carry, a tank, or a support mastermind?
DAY 3
TEAM PLAY
Synergy unlocked. Learning shot-calling, objective control, and playing around win conditions as a synchronized 5-man unit.
DAY 4
REAL LIFE SKILLS
Tilt management, effective communication under pressure, and healthy gaming habits. Translating game logic to life logic.
DAY 5
COMPETE
Tournament day. Put your training to the test in a structured bracket against other EKUZO camp teams.
