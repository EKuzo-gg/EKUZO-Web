THE PATHWAY // CURRICULUM

01
PHASE ONE

LEARN TO PLAY

Master the fundamentals. Map awareness, wave management, vision control, and economy. We break down the complex mechanics of League of Legends into digestible, actionable drills.
02
PHASE TWO

FIND YOUR ROLE

Top, Jungle, Mid, ADC, or Support. Students test out different positions and champion pools to find their specific playstyle and where they can best contribute to a unit.
03
PHASE THREE — CORE DIFFERENCE

TEAM PLAY & COMMS

This is where solo-queue ends. Students are locked into their 5-player squad. Focus shifts entirely to shot-calling, objective trading, drafting synergy, and positive communication under pressure.
04
PHASE FOUR

REAL LIFE SKILLS

Translating in-game leadership to the real world. Emotional regulation after a loss, giving constructive feedback, screen-time management, and understanding healthy competitive mindsets.
05
PHASE FIVE

COMPETE

Friday Tournament Day. The squad puts it all together in a structured, casted bracket against other camp teams. They leave the camp with a Discord group, a team name, and teammates for life.
