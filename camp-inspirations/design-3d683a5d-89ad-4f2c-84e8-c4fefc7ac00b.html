Key Differentiator

Take Your Team With You.

Most camps end on Friday, and the kids go their separate ways. At EKUZO, we place campers into 5-player squads on Day 1. You train together, compete together, and learn to communicate.

When camp ends, the squad remains. Campers leave not just with better skills, but with a reliable, non-toxic team to keep climbing the ranks with.

// Training Arc

The 5-Day Progression

01

Learn to Play [ MONDAY ]

Establishing the baseline. We cover advanced settings, CS fundamentals, wave management basics, and meta overview. Getting everyone on the same mechanical page.

02

Find Your Role [ TUESDAY ]

Champion specialization. Coaches break down positional responsibilities, matchups, and win conditions for Top, Jungle, Mid, ADC, and Support.

03

Team Play & Macro [ WEDNESDAY ]

Shifting from solo queue mentality to squad tactics. Objective control, vision lines, rotating, and effective comms without clutter.

04

Real Life Skills [ THURSDAY ]

Translating game sense to real life. We focus on emotional regulation (tilt management), constructive feedback, leadership, and digital citizenship.

05

Compete Together [ FRIDAY ]

Tournament day. Squads face off in a structured, casted bracket to apply everything they've learned in a high-stakes, supportive environment.
