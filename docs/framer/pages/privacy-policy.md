## Full Content

### Who We Are
**Legal name:** EKUZO Inc
**Entity type:** Delaware C Corporation
**Address:** 5617 Dolores Street, Houston, Texas 77057
**Website:** ekuzo.gg

### 2. Scope of This Policy
Applies to information collected through enrollment forms, live coaching sessions, the website (basic analytics), and email communications. EKUZO is a service-based program, not a consumer software platform.

### 3. Information We Collect

**A. Parent / Guardian Information**
- Full name, email, phone number
- Payment information (processed by third-party, not stored on EKUZO systems)
- Communication preferences

**B. Student Information**
- Full name, age range, games played
- Availability windows, team placement data, school name
- Coaching notes maintained for personalized development

**C. Live Session Data**
Programs use Discord for delivery. Sessions include voice and video participation and are recorded for safety, quality assurance, and coaching continuity. Recordings support coach training and accountability and are not used for advertising or unrelated purposes.

**D. Technical & Website Information**
- IP address, browser type, pages visited, general usage patterns via standard analytics and cookies

### 4. How We Use Information
Information supports program delivery, team placement, coaching, safety, communication, payment processing, and improvement. We do not sell personal information.

**A. Service Providers**
Trusted third parties include Discord, Stripe, and email infrastructure providers receiving only necessary information.

**B. Schools**
Relevant enrollment or participation information may be shared with partner schools consistent with parental consent.

**C. Legal or Safety Reasons**
Information may be disclosed to comply with legal obligations or to protect the safety of students, families, coaches, or the public.

### 6. Children's Privacy & Parental Role
Parents or guardians provide consent for participation. Students don't create independent consumer accounts. Coaches receive training for supervised minor environments.

### 7. Communications & Email Preferences
Communications include transactional messages (required), program updates, and optional newsletters. You may opt out of non-essential emails at any time using standard unsubscribe methods.

### 8. Cookies & Analytics
Basic cookies help understand website usage and improve performance. Browser settings allow cookie control without affecting program participation.

### 9. Data Retention
EKUZO retains records, including coaching notes and session recordings, for up to five (5) years to support safety, accountability, and consistent coaching records.

### 10. Data Security
Uses access controls, secure systems and vendors, and staff training and oversight. Protections are appropriate to program nature and minor involvement.

### 11. Parent Rights: Access, Updates, and Deletion
Parents may request access, correction, or deletion of information. Requests use the contact method below, subject to retention obligations.

### 12. U.S. Operations & California Privacy Rights
EKUZO operates primarily within the United States. California residents have additional rights. EKUZO does not sell personal information. Requests are handled per Section 11.

### 13. Media, Photos, and Recordings
EKUZO aims to obtain parental permission before public use of photos or clips. Session recordings serve internal purposes only. We do not sell or license student likenesses for advertising.

### 14. Changes to This Policy
Updates will be posted with revised dates. Continued participation indicates acceptance of changes.
