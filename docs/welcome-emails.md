# Welcome Email Templates — EKUZO

Three separate automated welcome emails, one per product.
Triggered on successful payment via Beehiiv automation.

---

## 1. EKUZO Camps — Welcome Email

**Subject:** You're in! Here's what's next for {gamer_name}'s camp week.

**Body:**

Hi {first_name},

You just did something great for your gamer. {gamer_name} is officially registered for EKUZO Camp — and we're excited to have them.

Here's what to expect:

**Before camp starts:**
- You'll receive a prep email 3 days before the session with login details, schedule, and what your gamer needs to have ready (game installed, headset, Discord).
- Make sure your gamer's preferred game is installed and updated.

**During camp:**
- Sessions run for a full week — your gamer will be placed on a team with a dedicated coach.
- Each day includes skill drills, team scrimmages, VOD review, and live coaching.
- You'll get a brief recap at the end of the week with your gamer's highlights.

**Have questions?**
Reply to this email or reach out at team@ekuzo.gg — we're real people and we respond fast.

Welcome to EKUZO.

— The EKUZO Team

## 2. EKUZO100 — Welcome Email

**Subject:** Welcome to EKUZO100 — {gamer_name}'s first team starts soon.

**Body:**

Hi {first_name},

{gamer_name} is officially enrolled in EKUZO100 — our 4-week intro program. One month, one team, one coach. Here's everything you need to know.

**What happens next:**
- We'll match {gamer_name} to a 5-player team based on age, skill, and game preference. You'll receive your team assignment and coach intro before the cohort starts.
- Sessions are twice a week, 90 minutes each. Days are flexible based on your cohort's availability — we'll confirm the exact schedule with you.

**Get ready:**
- Make sure your gamer has their preferred game installed and updated.
- A working headset with a mic is required — communication is a core part of the program.
- Discord is how we coordinate. If your gamer doesn't have an account, now's a good time to set one up.

**What to expect:**
- Week 1: Team formation, roles, first practice
- Week 2: Strategy sessions, team scrimmages
- Week 3: Competitive matches against other EKUZO teams
- Week 4: Final tournament, VOD review, growth report

**Have questions?**
Reply to this email or reach out at team@ekuzo.gg.

Welcome to the team.

— The EKUZO Team
